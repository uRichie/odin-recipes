Dickies Famous Mac n Cheese

Description

A smooth and creamy stovetop mac and cheese. Guaranteed to get you laid.

Ingredients

  • Your favourite pasta shape. So long as it's bronze die extruded, you're in the clear! My go to is oricheti but you do you.
  • Your favourite cheese - preferrably a good melter like mozza or cheddar.
  • Salt or stock powder.
  • A simple frozen veg so you don't get scruvy - I like to drop some pees in there.
  • Butter.

Steps

  1. The first step here is to understand that this ain't no run of the mill, boring ass mac and cheese. This is Dickie's Famous Mac n Cheese, and as such, there's going to be a special way of doing things.
  2. We're going to boil a kettle of water, more than you think you might need.
  3. From there, we're going to weigh out our pasta, 100-150 grams per person depending on how hungry you are.
  4. We're going to toss our weighed pasta onto the stove in a big pot with room for stirring.
  5. Once the water is boiling, put enough in the pot to cover the pasta, but no more! We can always add mroe later but we can't take it out.
  6. Crank the heat on the stove and start stirring - our low volume of water is going to make the pasta want to stick, we don't want that to happen.
  7. Continue stirring and adding water while the starches begin to form their own sauce.
  8. Once a starchy sauce has formed and the pasta is almost cooked through, toss in your butter.
  9. After the butter is melted, toss in your shredded cheese.
  10. Once the cheese is incorporated, toss in your frozen veg and continue to stir until they have thawed.
  11. Eat your tasty pasta.
