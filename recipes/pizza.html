Super Rad Pizza

Desciption

Cast iron pizza with a delicious tomato sauce topped with the finest mozzarela! This za is the bomb

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Mozzarela
  • Your favourite toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven as hot as it will go (~500F)
  2. Oil up your dough and stetch it to the shape of your cast iron skillet
  3. Toss some sauce on top of the dough
  4. Cheese is next - add your mozz and any other cheeses you like as well
  5. Throw your favourite toppings on top! Get real freaky with it
  6. Once your oven is preheated, toss the skillet in and give it about 12 minutes
  7. After abuot 12 minutes, the pizza should be golden brown on the bottom. Pull it out and let it rest or you'll fuck your mouth right up
  8. After about 5 minutes of cooling, release the pizza from the skillet, cut, and enjoy!
